UNOFFICIAL UK Parliament Data Spreadsheets

About

The UK Parliament makes Open Data available. However, it is available in a very technical form. This website tries to make it available in the more accessible form of spreadsheets, so that non-technical people can just grab it and go.

This website is COMPLETELY UNOFFICIAL and we make NO GUARANTEE about anything you may do with this data. USE AT YOUR OWN RISK.
